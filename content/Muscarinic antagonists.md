#Autonomic-modulators #Cholinergic-antagonists #Antimuscarinics 

## Mechanism of action

They inhibit M3 receptors on the detrusor muscle, inhibiting its contraction with acetylcholine.

## Adverse effects

- Flushing
- Accommodation problems
- Tachycardia
- Hyperactivity
- Dry mouth
- Residual urine
- Constipation
- Blurred vision
- Impaired cognition
- Glaucoma
